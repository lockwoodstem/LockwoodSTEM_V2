Passing Score

80%

Unlocks FabLab equipment/tool access

Overview

This certification verifies that you understand the essential skills, expectations, and evidence required for this area.

Required Skills

  • Explain the purpose of the skill or tool.
  • Follow safety and quality expectations.
  • Complete a practice task.
  • Submit certification evidence.

Study Guide

Review vocabulary, examples, procedures, and common mistakes before taking the practice test.

Practice Test

Use the practice test to check readiness before attempting the final certification test.

Final Test

Score at least 80% to pass. Retake expectations are set by the instructor.

Certificate Info

After passing, your certification can be recorded for class progress. Tool-access certifications may be used to determine supervised FabLab tool readiness.
